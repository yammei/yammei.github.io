Planning Development

May 09, 2024

Today is a pretty relaxing and productive day. I'm getting more ideas on what type of projects I want to work on. I think I'd love to make an app that can help women feel safer going out, but as to how I would accomplish that, I don't know yet.

I'm thought of some ideas like:

  • Group Walking: Finding other women to walk in groups with to a mutual destination.
  • Safety Alerts: Alerts authorities and nearby users about distressful and/or dangerous situations.
  • Safety Map: Allows users to recommend/review places that are known to be safe areas, and at what times they are safe.
I think they all could be valuable tools, and could even coexist into one app even. I'm still in the ideation phase of it all. So, there's no need to rush development yet, but I definitely could prepare the environment for it.

Last night, I saw someone on r/csmajors get VC backing after getting exhausted from applying for internships. This person was like a second year? Honestly, proud of them. It even inspired me to make tools that I believe could be useful. Though, locking an app for the safety of women behind a pay barrier is not it. So, this will remain just a personal project rather than going out and asking VCs to back me.

It'll be good experience creating more apps though. And I hope it'll actually be useful.
