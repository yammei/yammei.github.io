Automatic.

As a woman with a love for video games, but limited time to play, I have developed various automations to streamline time-gated sections. This way, I can allocate more time focusing on school, spending time with family, and... making more automations!

The genesis of this new found hobby starts with a scripting language for windows, AutoHotKey (AHK). One of the first few scripts written being a simple click action on loop for an indefinite time. And with time, came more complex scripts with responsive UI controls and dynamic image-processing logic.

Supersonic.

I moved on to stronger prototyping technologies such as Python, which allowed me to develop complex automation logic and integrate external libraries.

I've developed an automation with pre-determined logic proccessesing concurrently with image-to-text technology by Pytesseract. This automation allows me to work freely and save game content for later reading. I've constrained the reading bounds within a commonly located area to reduce external factors interference and processing time. Thus, optimizing the speed of the automation.

Hypnotic.

With the exponential boom of AI and the endless potential of machine learning, I've started to experiment with the many implementations of AI/ML in the realm of automation. And born is the idea of integrating OpenAI's gpt-3.5-turbo API with automation technology.

I've given my automations all access to game controls, and let normal automation processes run as normal. When text choices requiring player intervention occurs, the text choices are then sent to gpt-3.5-turbo to process. And having a pre-written prompt asking the AI to choose one of the many game controls to respond with, the AI control choices are then sent to the automation to execute.
