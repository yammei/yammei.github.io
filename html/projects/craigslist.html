A Strive for More

The Craigslist UX/UI redesign is my case study for Human-Centered Design class. Despite having already finished during Summer 2023, I felt as if more could have been improved. Thus, a further two month endeavor of redesign and user testing began.

Click here to read the original comprehensive case study.

Your Cup of Tea

I conducted qualitative research in the format of A/B testing, which has helped increase the overall site retention rate. Valuable user feedback is taken into consideration and UI adjustments are made to better user experience.

One example of a UI improvement made known by UI testers is the size of product cards. By building smaller playing-card-like views, users are able to view more products at a quicker rate. Thus, also implying quicker product information processing.

For All to See

Given Craigslist's large demographic reach. It is important, as it always has, to consider for user accessibility. As an individual with a family of inherited vision problems, we tend to have issues with webpages with seemingly indistinguishable color palletes. Thus, straining our eye muscles and causing an inability to decipher potentially important text.

By designing light and dark themes with sufficient contrast for text and various UI elements, we increase the inclusion of those with visual disabilities, and create a community where everyone can feel welcome.
