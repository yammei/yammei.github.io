My Soundtrack.

Picking out the right song just got a whole lot easier. With My Maestro, you will able to converse with an AI via the text message like environment. This allows the My Maestro Buddy to learn what songs would fit your mood and surroundings.

The conversations you have with My Maestro will feature seamless audio mixing for smooth song transitions. Thus, allowing the My Maestro Buddy to be as colorful and dynamic as your range of emotions.

My Friends.

My Maestro also features a social platform where you're able to check-in with how your friends are feeling.

Feeling super shy? You have the option to set your privacy to hide what you're listening to as well as the ability to set your online status as "Do not disturb". Looking to snapshot the mood? My Maestro will also allow you to save the current history of songs as a playlist for later listening!

My Growth.

My Maestro is a UX/UI case study to help me gain a deeper understanding of effective mobile UI design. A case study where I tackle questions to better empathize with the user such as: How can I provide easy-to-access features for a better user experience? What important considerations should be made in regard to mobile constraints? In which ways can I increase the perceived affordance of an element?

After multiple design revisions, qualitative user testing, and comparitive research, many UX/UI improvements have been made such as: optimized UI space usage by expanding the profile page UI to contain helpful information, provided easier touch accessibility by lowering music controls within thumbs reach, increased messaging UI familiarity by replacing the bottom navigation menu for a messaging box. And I'm now able to proudly deliver My Maestro as a successful concept of product.
