Smarter Music.

Picking out the right song just got a whole lot easier. The My Maestro buddy will analyze your mood and your music preferences to find the perfect song to listen to.

The conversations you have with My Maestro will feature seamless audio mixing for smooth song transitions. Thus, allowing the My Maestro Buddy to be as colorful and dynamic as your range of emotions.

My Friends.

My Maestro doubles as a social platform to share what your listening to with your friends. One way being the option to display your favorite artist and give them the recognition they deserve!

Feeling super shy? No worries! You have the option to set your privacy to hide what you're listening to as well as the ability to set your online status as "Do not disturb".

My Growth.

My Maestro is a UX/UI case study to help me gain a deeper understanding of effective mobile UI design. A case study where I learn to better empathize with user-accessibility and important considerations in regard to mobile UI constraints?

After multiple design revisions, user-testing, and research, many UX/UI improvements have been made to make My Maestro more user-friendly!
